01 — Manifesto

We don't just talk about change. We make it happen.

Skyrocketing costs. Clinician burnout. Patient confusion. A reactive system that emphasizes complexity over prevention. The Boombostic Health podcast is a movement for people who know healthcare can — and must — do better.

01 / Costs

Price opacity is a choice, not a physics problem.

We pressure-test the business models that profit from complexity — and spotlight the operators replacing them with something honest.

02 / Burnout

Clinicians shouldn't subsidize a broken workflow with their sanity.

AI, ambient capture, and rebuilt care teams are already giving hours back. We talk to the people shipping it at scale.

03 / Outcomes

Reactive care is a failure of imagination.

Prevention is not a wellness slogan — it's the P&L. Hear from the founders turning predictive data into paid interventions.

If you fear change and are looking for safe, surface-level conversations — this isn't for you. We're here to eliminate the status quo.
— Bradley Bostic · Host & Founder
