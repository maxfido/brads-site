02 · About the Host

Bradley Bostic.

Entrepreneur · Investor · Inventor · Podcaster. 25 years building high-growth healthtech. Inventor on 30+ patents. Portfolio serving 100M+ lives.

Bradley Bostic Founder & Host
Indianapolis, IN
"The future of healthcare delivery isn't reactive. It's predictive. It's personal. It's here. The time is now for bold leaders to harness the power of AI to transform it." — Bradley Bostic

Bradley Bostic has spent 25 years building technology companies at the intersection of data and healthcare. He has founded, funded, and operated multiple healthtech businesses focused on improving clinical and financial outcomes.

The loss of his mother to colon cancer at 56 was a pivotal moment, inspiring him to address the inefficiencies in the healthcare system he encountered early in his career. This personal tragedy fueled his drive to innovate and improve patient outcomes.

His portfolio companies apply AI and machine learning to clinical data, medication management, and device safety — reaching over 100 million people across the US.
